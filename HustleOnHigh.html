Info

Game Concept

Hustle on High is a fast-paced roguelike where clearing rooms from enemies quickly is a must. The game’s core feature where speed is everything, even health, adds a unique spin to dungeon crawler and roguelike genres! It also offers a multiplayer experience, fight with friends to clear rooms efficiently, or fight your friends to prove yourself the faster demon!

Story

An infernal realm is threatened by a human invasion seeking to harvest a vital resource native to this land. They hope it will solve Earth’s energy problems and are willing to destroy this world to do it. The player’s character is a crazy, deranged inhabitant of this place, who has always wanted to join its military forces to defend his home. Unfortunately, he is not nearly as strong as his brother, and for that, they mocked and ridiculed him. However, what he lacks in strength, he compensates with lightning speed. In an attempt to sneak into one of the platoons to aid in the ongoing war, the character stumbles through an interdimensional portal. He finds himself in a lab holding the only weapon that could ultimately bring his world to an end. Knowing now that the army is doomed to fail, and with no way to warn them, the fate of his people rests on his shoulders. He, along with a new friend, must fight against countless foes sent forth by the lab and its directors; using his speed, his lack of self-preservation, and anything and everything he finds along the way.
